**Orange Line** ![The Southwest Corridor heading east - Photo from Blue Bikes, photo links](https://d21xlh2maitm24.cloudfront.net/bst/southwest-corridor.png?mtime=20180503112000) # Overview The Southwest Cooridor is a hidden treasure of Boston. Stay on the running side of the divded path and you should be safe from bikes. Highlights include Reggie Lewis Indoor Track, Northeastern, and the Orange Line itself. Bonus points for running faster than the T. The naming convention is driven by the imfamous Orange Line run. Forward is defined as Mass Ave first.
