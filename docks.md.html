**Docks** ![Looking back on the Docks - Photo from TV News Check, photo links](https://media.tvnewscheck.com/wp/content/uploads/sites/3/2019/06/Wynn-Boston-Harbor-progress.jpg) # Overview Do you know what I am hearing? A lot of Docks. This is the classic run in Boston. It goes past great Boston landmarks like the North End, the Freedom Trail, Quincy Market, the Boston Common, and of course, Docks! For all of those non coastal kids out there, docks are those things that are on the edge of the water. They are fun to run along and give great views. The run is almost completely flat but also all concrete. Soft surfaces are out there, one just needs to be creative. The naming convention is driven by Docks. This is a legendary run that is traditionally done as a first run in Boston. There is some contention on what is truly docks. I will admit that the full docks is more accurately locks, but it is far superior so it bears the name. [Home](http://web.mit.edu/sgoldy/www/routes/cambridge/routes_cambridge.md.html) # Variations 1.
