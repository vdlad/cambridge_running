**Charleston** ![The Charleston Navy Yard - Photo from National Park Service, photo links](https://www.nps.gov/common/uploads/grid_builder/bost/crop16_9/056B83F2-1DD8-B71B-0BD56876F2149512.jpg) # Overview Where do you go when the river gets boring but you are not brave enough to navigate the North end. Head towards Battle Docks! Charleston is a fun area to explore with notable destinations of Bunker Hill, Charleston Navy yard and Ryan Playground. With the exception of Bunker hill everything is pretty flat. Most of it is concrete but there are some oppurtunites for dirt side trails and wooden docks. The Navy yard gets plowed often and there is decent lighting throughout (with the possible exception of Chelsea St) The naming convention originates from the seed runs Castle and Battle Docks. While these runs may not be frame perfect anymore, the spirit is the same.
