**Reservoir** ![Chestnut Hill Reservoir on a cool summer evening - Photo from Boston Magazine, photo links](https://www.bostonmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/07/chestnut-hill.jpg) # Overview Boston has 2 hills, Bunker Hill and Heartbreak Hill. This is the closest we get to the latter. The Chestnut Hill Reservoir is a joy to run around, one has a good chance of seeing Boston College or Boston University teams. On Marathon Monday this is the place to be. Get slurpees by the rez then trace the course all the way to the finish. The naming convention is driven by the main body of water: the Rez. Forward is defined as BU first. [Home](http://web.mit.edu/sgoldy/www/routes/cambridge/routes_cambridge.md.html) # Variations This area is kind of a reach so loops tend to be longer. 1.
