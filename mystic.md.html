# Overview Some say the Mystic River is the ugly step child of the Boston rivers, some say it is the promised land. I leave it up to you to figure out who is right. Everything is pretty flat but unfortunately there are not many soft surface oppurtunities. There are some rough concrete jungle spots, but there are some treasures: Lego Land, the yellow brick road of the Encore Casino, and of course the Mystic. The naming convention is driven by the main body of water: the Mystic River. This is an under utilized area, for that reason a forward direction has not been determined.
